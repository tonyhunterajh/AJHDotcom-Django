Ministry Bio

Rev. Anthony J. Hunter is an evangelist, teacher and author devoted to the ministry of preaching the gospel of Jesus Christ, teaching the word of God, and reaching the world for Christ. He is also a former youth pastor, as well as a former senior pastor who is extremely proud of the amazing work he was assigned by God. Rev. Hunter is a full-time Information Technology Consultant. He is married to Demetria D. Hunter his wife, life partner and best friend. Rev. Hunter and his wife have five children and 12 grandchildren.

I have been in church my entire life. I was raised to worship and praise God, to serve Him with all that I am and to love Jesus above all else. In spite of the amazing upbringing I received, as a young man I went astray and lived a life completely unacceptable in God's eyes. I drank, used drugs and lived almost every aspect of my life outside the will of God.

In 1985, my life took a much needed turn for the better but, only after a terrible turn for the worse! after a night of riotous living, I found myself walking the streets of Indianapolis in a stupor. While I was walking, I heard a voice that asked: "what are you doing to my temple?". I heard this 3 times, and on the third time the high I had was instantly gone! I ran home, pulled out my personal bible, the big family bible and another bible. I lay on the floor searching the Word of God for anything that would speak to me about my life. That's when I found...

Isaiah 9:6

For unto us a child is born, unto us a son is given: and the government shall be upon his shoulder: and his name shall be called Wonderful, Counsellor, The mighty God, The everlasting Father, The Prince of Peace.

In 1986, I truly found Jesus and received Him as my Lord and Saviour. Nothing brings me more joy in life than knowing Jesus and having an authentic relationship with my Father through Him.
